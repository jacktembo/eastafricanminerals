Specify your requirements — volume, form (bars, doré, nuggets), purity, and timeline. We'll match you with verified, licensed sellers and provide pricing and documentation within 24 hours.
2. What forms of gold are available?
We offer gold bullion bars (1kg, 10oz, 400oz), doré bars, raw nuggets, and gold concentrate. All products come with assay certificates, chain-of-custody documentation, and compliance records.
3. What is the minimum order quantity?
Minimum orders vary by product type and supplier. Typically, bullion bars start from 1kg. For doré and concentrate, minimum volumes depend on the specific seller. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
4. How is gold pricing determined?
Pricing is based on the London Bullion Market Association (LBMA) spot price, adjusted for purity, form, and logistics. We provide transparent price benchmarking to ensure you get fair market rates.
How is gold shipped and what security measures are in place?
All shipments use armored vehicles with trained escorts for ground transport. International shipments are coordinated with specialized precious metals freight forwarders, fully insured, with complete chain-of-custody documentation.
What payment methods do you accept?
We support wire transfers (SWIFT/SEPA), letters of credit (LC), and escrow arrangements for verified buyers. Payment terms are agreed on a per-transaction basis based on buyer qualification.
