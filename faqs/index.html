BUYING GOLD 


Start by submitting an inquiry via WhatsApp or our contact form. Specify your requirements — volume, form (bars, doré, nuggets), purity, and timeline. We'll match you with verified, licensed sellers and provide pricing and documentation within 24 hours.

We offer gold bullion bars (1kg, 10oz, 400oz), doré bars, raw nuggets, and gold concentrate. All products come with assay certificates, chain-of-custody documentation, and compliance records.

Minimum orders vary by product type and supplier. Typically, bullion bars start from 1kg. For doré and concentrate, minimum volumes depend on the specific seller. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

Pricing is based on the London Bullion Market Association (LBMA) spot price, adjusted for purity, form, and logistics. We provide transparent price benchmarking to ensure you get fair market rates. 

COMPLIANCE & DOCUMENTATION

LOGISTICS & EXPORT 


All shipments use armored vehicles with trained escorts for ground transport. International shipments are coordinated with specialized precious metals freight forwarders, fully insured, with complete chain-of-custody documentation.

We currently operate export corridors from Uganda, Democratic Republic of Congo, Ghana, Tanzania, South Africa, and other key producing nations. We handle all customs clearance and export licensing.

From first inquiry to delivery, transactions typically take 7–21 business days depending on volume, destination, and compliance requirements. Expedited processing is available for pre-qualified buyers.

Yes. All shipments are covered by comprehensive cargo insurance from pickup to final delivery. Insurance certificates are provided as part of the full transaction documentation pack..

PAYMENTS & PRICING


We support wire transfers (SWIFT/SEPA), letters of credit (LC), and escrow arrangements for verified buyers. Payment terms are agreed on a per-transaction basis based on buyer qualification.

Our advisory team can help structure pricing terms and evaluate hedging instruments to manage price volatility. This is available to buyers with established relationships and sufficient volumes.

ASSAYING & QUALITY


All gold is assayed by accredited laboratories using fire assay or XRF methods. Assay reports detail purity, weight, and composition. We also support independent third-party assaying at the buyer's request.

We supply gold ranging from 22 karat (91.6%) alluvial nuggets to 999.9 fine gold bullion bars. Doré typically ranges from 70–95% purity depending on origin. All purity is certified by assay.
